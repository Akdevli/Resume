How long does a website take to build?

Website development time is highly variable, depending on project scope, design complexity, features, and client feedback.  I work closely with clients to define a clear process, from initial discovery and planning to design, development, testing, and launch, ensuring a realistic timeline and budget tailored to their specific needs.  Contact me to discuss your project and receive a personalized estimate

How much does a website cost?

Website pricing is project-specific, influenced by factors like scope, design, functionality, and content.  I prioritize a collaborative discovery process to understand your unique needs and goals, enabling me to deliver a transparent and tailored quote.

Will my website load quickly?

I employ a range of techniques to ensure your website loads quickly and efficiently, including optimized code, image compression, efficient caching strategies, and leveraging Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) where appropriate.  I also conduct thorough performance testing throughout the development process to identify and address any potential bottlenecks
